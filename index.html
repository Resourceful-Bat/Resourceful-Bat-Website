CollabQuest Group Project

Much more complicated than primary school group projects.

One of the more intense projects, I worked with a team to create a website called CollabQuest. CollabQuest gives Pitt students a place to join/form group projects. The name's inspiration comes from quest boards that you can find in RPGs. Similar to how quest boards gather parties to take on a quest, CollabQuest gathers students hoping to gain project experience.
